My favourite drama

Crayon Shin-chan is my favourite drama.This drama mainly revolves around a five-year-old kindergarten student.
I like Crayon Shin-chan because the characters in it are distinct and have independent personalities. Shin-chan is a funny and considerate character. Every drama is a little touched while being funny.

The family living with the protagonist has:

  • Shinnosuke Nohara (Protagonist)
  • Hiroshi Nohara(Protagonist's father)
  • Misae Nohara(Protagonist's mother)
  • Himawari Nohara(Protagonist's younger sister)
  • Shiro Nohara(Protagonist's pet dog)
Friends who have a good relationship with the protagonist:
  1. Kazama Toru
  2. Nene Sakurada
  3. Masao Sato
  4. Bo - Chan
